### Platform Type
**Multi-Service Aggregator Platform** - Sports Vidya operates as an integrated ecosystem connecting various sports service providers with athletes, aspiring sportspersons, and sports enthusiasts.

Sports Vidya operates on a **Partnership & Commission-Based Model**:

Sports Vidya positions itself as the **world's first platform integrating nearly every essential component** needed to develop truly world-class, well-educated sportspersons. The platform eliminates the fragmentation in sports services by bringing everything under one roof.

## PROBLEM AREAS SPORTS VIDYA ADDRESSES

### Systemic Issues in Indian Sports (As identified by the company)

1. **Poor Coach Compensation:**
   - Low salaries force coaches to seek alternative income
   - Creates inequality in athlete selection and training
   - Talented children often left behind

2. **Infrastructure Challenges:**
   - Poor maintenance of existing facilities
   - Lack of utilization planning
   - Insufficient world-class training centers

3. **Coaching Quality Gaps:**
   - Lack of specialization (different coaches needed for different events/disciplines)
   - Confusion about beginner vs. intermediate vs. advanced vs. professional level coaching
   - Insufficient understanding of sport-specific requirements

4. **Nutrition & Science Gaps:**
   - Standardized nutrition without DNA-based customization
   - Inadequate supplement provision
   - Low-quality mess food for athletes

5. **Equipment Issues:**
   - Prevalence of duplicate/fake sports equipment
   - Equipment purchased but not distributed to athletes
   - Lack of access to latest innovations

6. **Administrative Inefficiencies:**
   - File pushing between departments
   - Training schedule disruptions due to VIP visits
   - Political and religious interference in sports

7. **Financial Barriers:**
   - High GST rates on sports goods (5-18%)
   - High GST on sports nutrition (18%)
   - Limited financial support for athletes

8. **Medal & Trophy Quality:**
   - Low-quality fiber trophies and medals
   - Doesn't reflect the effort and achievement of athletes

9. **Exploitation of Athletes:**
   - Renowned athletes offered low-profile jobs
   - Dronacharya Awardees underpaid
   - Lack of standardized compensation structures
